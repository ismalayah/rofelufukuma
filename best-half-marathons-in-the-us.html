Best Half Marathons In The US, Distance And location

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Tuesday, May 28, 2024

4. Napa to Sonoma Wine Country Half Marathon – Sonoma, California

The Napa to Sonoma Wine Country Half Marathon offers a point-to-point course that meanders through rolling hills and takes you past some of California’s most renowned vineyards.

The race commences at Cuvaison Carneros Winery, creating a stunning starting point in one of the most beautiful locations.

Throughout the run, participants are treated to magnificent views of the Napa-Sonoma Marshes Wildlife Area, San Pablo Bay, and the Marin County hills.

Every finisher is rewarded with a race shirt, a finisher’s medal, and an etched wine glass, along with the chance to partake in an enjoyable post-race celebration.
