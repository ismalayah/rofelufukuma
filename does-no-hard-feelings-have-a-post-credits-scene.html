Does No Hard Feelings have a post-credits scene?

Posted by Martina Birk on Saturday, April 27, 2024

No Hard Feelings has officially landed in theaters and subsequently shown its hand as of today, proving quite dashingly that one should never bet against Jennifer Lawrence and her ability to pick out something special. Indeed, the sex comedy’s premise is nothing short of uncomfortable, which may be more than enough to put some people off from taking the plunge, but those who give it a chance just might be pleasantly surprised at the film’s tender, important message, and lens, powered in part by Lawrence’s infallible charm.

Recommended Videos

Inspired by a real Craigslist ad sent to writer-director Gene Stupnitsky by a pair of the film’s producers, No Hard Feelings stars Lawrence (who also produces) as Maddie, a struggling, 32-year-old Uber driver whose car gets repossessed as property taxes on her inherited childhood home continue to go unpaid. As she scours the web for solutions, she happens upon a couple, Allison and Laird Becker, who offer her a used car in exchange for dating Percy, their 19-year-old, introverted, sexually-disinterested son, so as to bring him out of his shell, as it were.

Of course, No Hard Feelings is a movie released in 2023, so the main question on everyone’s mind is whether or not there’s a post-credits scene in play. Can’t risk sitting through the names of the people who made the movie without that extra payoff, right?

Is there a post-credits scene in No Hard Feelings?

No, there is no post-credits scene or mid-credits scene in No Hard Feelings. It’s no surprise that this is the case, of course; no one should be going into this movie expecting it to set up any sort of shared universe, even if the thought is equal parts hilarious and uncanny.

No Hard Feelings is now playing in cinemas.
