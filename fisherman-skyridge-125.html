Fisherman (Japanese: 釣り人 Fisherman) is a Supporter card. It was first released as part of the Skyridge expansion.

Card text

Skyridge print
You can play only one Supporter card each turn. When you play this card, put it next to your Active Pokémon. When your turn ends, discard this card.
Choose 4 basic Energy cards from your discard pile (if there are fewer basic Energy cards than choose, take all of them), show them to your opponent, and put them into your hand.
HeartGold & SoulSilver print
You can play only one Supporter card each turn. When you play this card, put it next to your Active Pokémon. When your turn ends, discard this card.
Search your discard pile for 4 basic Energy cards, show them to your opponent, and put them into your hand.
BREAKthrough print
Put 4 basic Energy cards from your discard pile into your hand.
You may play only 1 Supporter card during your turn (before your attack).
Pokémon Trading Card Game Classic print
Put up to 4 Basic Energy cards from your discard pile into your hand.
You may play only 1 Supporter card during your turn.

e-Reader data

The Skyridge print includes a Dot Code strip for use with the e-Reader, and has the card ID F-47-#. The short strip contains a brief card summary, and information on the content of the long strip.

Construction: Action

The long strip contains player data for the Construction: Action application.

Release information

This card was included in the Skyridge expansion with artwork by Kagemaru Himeno, first released in the Japanese Mysterious Mountains expansion. It was reprinted in the HeartGold & SoulSilver expansion with new artwork by Kanako Eo, first released in the Japanese SoulSilver Collection. It was reprinted again in the BREAKthrough expansion with artwork by Hitoshi Ariga, first released in the Japanese Raichu BREAK Evolution Pack. It was reprinted once again in the Celestial Storm expansion with artwork by Masakazu Fukuda, first released in the Japanese Sky-Splitting Charisma expansion. A Full Art print with artwork also by Masakazu Fukuda was included in the Hidden Fates expansion, first released in the Japanese GX Ultra Shiny subset.

In the Scarlet & Violet Series, the BREAKthrough print of Fisherman was reprinted in Holofoil for the Blastoise & Suicune ex deck of the Pokémon Trading Card Game Classic. This reprint lacks a regulation mark and is thus not legal for tournament play.

Trivia

The intended message of the card on the Skyridge print was "If there are fewer basic Energy cards than that, choose all of them."

Origin

The Fisherman Trainer class is a type of Pokémon Trainer in the Pokémon games. The HeartGold & SoulSilver print features a Fisherman as they appear in the Generation IV games, the BREAKthrough print features a Fisherman as they appear in Pokémon X and Y, and the Celestial Storm and Hidden Fates prints feature a Fisherman as they appear in Pokémon Sun and Moon and Pokémon Ultra Sun and Ultra Moon.
