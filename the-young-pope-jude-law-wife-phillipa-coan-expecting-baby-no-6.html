The Young Pope Actor, Jude Law Ready for Baby No. 6! First For his New Wife Phillipa Coan

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Thursday, July 4, 2024

With Baby Number 6, The Young Pope actor Jude Law is celebrating early Father's Day. The actor's wife Phillipa Coan is pregnant as per reports and the paparazzi spotted Coan in London with a baby bump.

During an interview with The Telegraph, Jude Law said,

"I'm fortunate to be with someone where I'm having more fun than I've ever had in my life. We have an incredibly stable and healthy, wonderful family existence."

The actor, further, revealed how thrilled he will be to welcome baby number six with his wife. Prior to this, Spy actor already shares five children from his three different relationships.

From his ex-wife Sadie Frost, Law is the father to Rafferty Law (23), Iris Law (19), and Rudy Law (17). Not just that, with former partner Samantha Burke, the actor shares one kid, Sophia Law (10). He's also welcomed Ada Law (5) with his former partner Catherine Harding.

On his romance with Coan and wanting to be a father to baby number six, the actor says,

"And that involves my kids who are young adults... and then the younger ones are just so much joy and so much fun. I love it, so absolutely, why not have another? I'm very lucky to be involved with someone I'm madly in love with. So the idea of having more children would be just wonderful."

Apart from the good news of Phillipa Coan's pregnancy, the couple also recently celebrated their first wedding anniversary on May 1. The lovebirds started dating in 2015 and walked down the aisle on May 1, 2019. 

For the big day, the bride appeared in a short, ruched cream dress, with a pair of nude pink heels and a white fascinator. On the other hand, the groom exchanged wedding vows in his velvet blue suit with a white v-neck underneath and suede shoes of a lighter shade of blue.

The Spy actor has also dated American Sniper actress Sienna Miller after his first divorce in 2003. The duo's relationship, however, ended in 2006 after Jude started having an affair with his children's nanny.
